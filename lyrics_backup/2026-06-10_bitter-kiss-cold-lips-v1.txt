[Hook]
I can still taste the shadow of your smile
Linger in the dark for a little while
We are dancing on the edge of the night
Bitter kiss, cold lips, but the fire is bright

[Verse 1]
The streetlamps flicker outside the door
While we leave our footprints across the floor
I trace the memories etched in your skin
Wondering where you end and I begin

[Pre-Chorus]
The shadows are long and the hour is late
I am holding the moment, willing to wait
Your eyes are a promise I am dying to keep
The water is rising, the current is deep

[Chorus]
